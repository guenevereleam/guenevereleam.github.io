Digital Art

I have been creating digital art since 2020, when my parents gave me an iPad and Apple Pencil for my birthday. Since then, I've made thousands of digital drawings on Procreate, which takes up about 30GB of space on my iPad.

Because I've made literally thousands of pieces (most of which are not fit for the human e) I am sharing only a small selection of the works I am the most happy with.

This is by far still the largest of any of my collections, even though I cut it down drastically. Because of that, most of them won't have a ton of description, if any. Most of them are also not going to be titled. However, with the magic of technology, I do know the dates of each piece, and how long each took, so I will include that if nothing else!

The art is (more-or-less) displayed with newest towards the top and old ones towards the bottom.

A short game created for HackUMass 2025. Two friends and I worked together to create the game from scratch in the allotted 36 hours. We collaborated to create the story, and then split the rest of the workload among us. I was repsonsible for all of the art shown in the game, including sprites and backgrounds. I did not know I would be doing this project until last minute, so all ideas were off-the-cuff and we had no time to brainstorm in advance.
